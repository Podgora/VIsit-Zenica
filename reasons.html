Welcome To Zenica!

Bosnia and Hercegovina

History

Stećci are a folk custom and artistic creation of a certain area in a certain time. They began to be carved and installed from the end of the 12th to the end of the 16th century. The Vranduk fortress was built at the end of the 14th century...

Architecture

A city with a long and rich history marked by the collision of the architecture of the Ottoman Empire, the Austro-Hungarian monarchy, the communist way of building and modern urbanism.

Sport

Home of the national team, Bilino Polje stadium in the heart of the city. As well as the club FK.Celik, which has a rich European football history. Next to one of the most modern training centers in Europe.

Food

Among the many restaurants, it is difficult to single out some in particular, but the Dubrovnik restaurant, Salčinović restaurant and Bešlić restaurant stand out due to their tradition.

Relax and explore

There are several parks in the very center of the city. The best for sports relaxation or just enjoying nature is Kamberovica Polje. You can also visit the nearby excursion sites Smetovi, Bistricak... or explore and find many more in the surrounding hills.

Some more details

Zenica is also marked by two large institutions: Zeljezara Zenica (steel factory) and KPD Zenica (penitentiary).
